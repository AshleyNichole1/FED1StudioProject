Heather R. Bollinger, DO
Undergraduate Missouri State University Post-Graduate Missouri State University State Licensure Licensed Professional Counselor Professional Areas of Interest – Anna has worked in a variety of settings including the military, inpatient and outpatient treatment centers, domestic violence centers, crisis intervention and an adoption agency. Additionally, she is a certified Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator Practitioner. She works with individuals who have experienced trauma, struggle with communication and/or have mood, attachment or anxiety disorders. Anna uses a client-centered approach to counseling with an eclectic style that adapts to the unique needs of each client. Anna's framework is based on the belief that everyone has value, purpose and something beautiful to offer. Her goal is to navigate and help you discovery the peace, joy and happiness in the life you are meant to live. Personal Information – In her spare time, Anna loves to spend time with her family, good friends, and long walks with her dog.
