Class Subjects

CNA, EKG, Phlebotomy
These short, structured online and hybrid programs will help get you certified in EKG - monitoring the cardiovascular system, phlebotomy - procuring blood samples, or gerontology - the study of aging.
LPN to RN
Nursing AdLib's LPN to RN program allows you to quickly take your education to the next level. As an LPN, you can get credit by examination for some professional coursework, meaning you'll finish your associate degree faster.
Radiation Therapy
Over two years, you'll travel to several clinical affiliates, each with different types of patients and equipment, studying the principles of radiation and how they're applied to developing treatment plans.
Associate of Science in Nursing
Nursing AdLib's associate degree in nursing (ASN) allows you to make an impact quickly in your new healthcare career. In just two years, you will gain the hands-on knowledge and technical skills needed to become a registered nurse.
RN to BSN
You're already a great nurse; a bachelor's degree in nursing can open even more doors. If you’re thinking about a BSN we can transfer your existing coursework and finish only what you need to complete your RN-BSN!
HealthCare Administration
Nursing AdLib's Bachelor of Science in Healthcare Administration degree is a flexible, online/in person bachelor completion* program. If you're already a health professional, it can help you advance your career and earnings to the next level.
